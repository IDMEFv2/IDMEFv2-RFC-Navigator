Attributes

IP (Optional)

Target IP address.

Hostname (Optional)

Hostname of this target.

Note

Expires 18 April 2023 [Page 41]

Service (Optional)

Service or process impacted by the alert.

User (Optional)

User ID or login targeted by the alert.

Email (Optional)

Email address targeted by the alert.

Port (Optional)

Target ports involved in the alert.

GeoLocation (Optional)

GPS coordinates for the target.

UnLocation (Optional)

Standard UN/Locode for the target.

Location (Optional)

Internal name for the location of the target.

Attachment (Optional)

Identifiers for attachments related to this target.

Observable (Optional)

Identifiers for observables related to this target.
